Lemon Tea

  • 2 CUPS of Water
  • One lemon
  • Tea powder
  1. In a pan, heat water on high heat
  2. Once the water comes to rolling boil, squeeze in lemon juice, add sugar and switch off heat. Keep the container covered for a minute.
  3. Stir to dissolve any demerara sugar that is still left at the bottom. Strain the tea, pour to tea cups
